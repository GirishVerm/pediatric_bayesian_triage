# Symptoms in Pediatric.db - Summary

## Total Count
**812 unique symptoms** (phenotypes) are stored in the database.

## Complete List
See `ALL_SYMPTOMS.txt` for the complete alphabetical list of all 812 symptoms.

## Categories (Sample)

### Respiratory Symptoms (~50+)
- Cough, Wheezing, Dyspnea, Tachypnea, Stridor, Chest retractions, etc.

### Gastrointestinal Symptoms (~40+)
- Abdominal pain, Vomiting, Diarrhea, Constipation, Nausea, etc.

### ENT Symptoms (~30+)
- Ear pain, Sore throat, Nasal congestion, Otorrhea, etc.

### Skin Symptoms (~60+)
- Rash (various types), Erythema, Pruritus, Vesicular lesions, Crusts, etc.

### Neurological Symptoms (~40+)
- Headache, Seizures, Neurological deficits, Ataxia, Weakness, etc.

### Cardiovascular Symptoms (~30+)
- Heart murmur, Arrhythmia, Chest pain, Cyanosis, etc.

### Genitourinary Symptoms (~20+)
- Dysuria, Urinary frequency, Urinary urgency, Hematuria, etc.

### Eye Symptoms (~15+)
- Eye redness, Eye discharge, Conjunctivitis, etc.

### Other Categories
- Age-specific symptoms
- Laboratory findings
- Imaging findings
- Genetic markers
- Behavioral symptoms
- Developmental symptoms

## Usage

All symptoms are stored in the `phenotypes` table and linked to diseases via `disease_phenotype_evidence` with:
- Likelihood ratios (LR+, LR-)
- Sensitivity and Specificity
- Source citations (PMIDs)
